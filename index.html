Los Angeles

Los Angeles, often referred to as L.A., is a sprawling metropolis located in Southern California. Known for its Mediterranean climate, diverse culture, and as a global center for the entertainment industry, it is a city that has something to offer everyone. With a population of nearly 4 million people, it is the second-most populous city in the United States, after New York City. The city is characterized by its vast size, iconic landmarks, and a unique blend of cultures that contribute to its rich tapestry.
